# Assist Pro: Capability Catalog 💎

Assist Pro empowers your team through deep integrations with the platforms you use most. Here is an overview of our current high-level capabilities.

---

### 📧 Google Workspace (Gmail & Calendar)
Turn your inbox and schedule into an automated powerhouse.
- **Email Orchestration**: Send, draft, and read emails using context-aware AI.
- **Dynamic Scheduling**: Create, update, and list calendar events through simple natural language commands.
- **Team Coordination**: Automatically sync schedules across workspaces.

### 📊 Google Sheets
Automate your data entry and reporting 24/7.
- **Data Insertion**: Add rows to specific spreadsheets with mapped headers.
- **Inventory & Tracking**: Use AI to organize spreadsheets and find specific documents by name.
- **Sheet Creation**: Command the AI to initialize new spreadsheets for specific projects.

### 💬 Slack Integration
Keep your team informed without ever leaving the dashboard.
- **Channel Communication**: Post updates directly to public or private channels.
- **Discovery**: Automatically list and identify relevant channels for specific project updates.

### 📓 Notion
Transform your workspaces into a self-organizing knowledge base.
- **Page Generation**: Create meeting notes, SOPs, or project updates in specific Notion databases.
- **Database Discovery**: Identify and map Notion databases for automated logging.

### 🧡 HubSpot CRM
Maintain a high-velocity sales pipeline with AI lead management.
- **Lead Intake**: Add contacts directly to your CRM from any external trigger.
- **Lead Research**: List and identify recent activity within your CRM contacts.

---

## 🛡️ The "Human-in-the-Loop" Guarantee

To ensure absolute reliability, Assist Pro includes:
1. **Approval Gates**: Sensitive actions (like sending emails) always require a simple "Approve" before execution.
2. **Task Logs**: Every AI execution is logged as a trackable task with success/fail status and output summaries.
3. **Live Notifications**: Immediate feedback on every action, accessible via the dashboard or mobile.
