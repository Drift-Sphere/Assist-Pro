# Assist Pro: Capability Catalog 💎

Assist Pro empowers your team through deep integrations with the platforms you use most. Here is an overview of our current high-level capabilities.

---

### 🧠 Advanced AI & Memory
The core of Assist Pro's intelligence.
- **Multi-Model Routing**: Automatically shifts between GPT-4o, Claude 3.5, and fast Llama-3 depending on task complexity.
- **RAG Active Memory**: Retains long-term context from past tasks and conversations.
- **Digital Twin**: Learns and mimics your exact drafting tone from uploaded documents or past emails.
- **Voice-to-Task (STT)**: Direct voice transcription and execution via specialized audio models.

### 🌐 Autonomous Web & CRM
- **Autonomous Browser Agent**: Can navigate websites, read content, and extract data to accomplish open-ended research goals.
- **Proactive Opportunity Spotter**: Background cron jobs can monitor news and trigger automated engagement scripts.
- **HubSpot, Pipedrive, & Zoho**: Full CRM integration for contact management, lead creation, and activity logging.

### 📧 Workspace & Communication
Turn your inbox and standard tools into an automated powerhouse.
- **Google Workspace (Gmail, Calendar, Sheets)**: Advanced orchestration for email sending, dynamic scheduling, and data insertion.
- **Slack & Twilio (WhatsApp)**: Send direct messages or channel updates effortlessly.

### 📓 Organization & Productivity
Transform your workspaces into a self-organizing knowledge base.
- **Notion**: Create meeting notes, SOPs, or project updates in specific databases.
- **Trello & ClickUp**: Automatically create cards, tasks, and update board statuses based on project parameters.
- **Zoom & Calendly**: Automated meeting generation and event discovery.

---

## 🛡️ The "Human-in-the-Loop" Guarantee

To ensure absolute reliability, Assist Pro includes:
1. **Approval Gates**: Sensitive actions (like sending emails) always require a simple "Approve" before execution.
2. **Task Logs**: Every AI execution is logged as a trackable task with success/fail status and output summaries.
3. **Live Notifications**: Immediate feedback on every action, accessible via the dashboard or mobile.
